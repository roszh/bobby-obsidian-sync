# Gentle&Rose Bulgaria

## Positioning
Natural, clean, effective skincare with a soft premium feel. Emphasis on gentle care, hydration, glow, anti-aging support, and harmony with the skin.

## Audience
Women 40+ appear to be a strong core audience, consistent with Ros's broader business context.

## Core Products
- Hydrating Cleanser / Хидратираща почистваща Пяна с Розова Вода
- Pure Rose Water / Органична Розова Вода
- Rose Youth Elixir / Розов Еликсир на Младостта
- Prebiotic Moisturising Cream / Пребиотичен Хидратиращ Крем
- Antarctic Sun Defence SPF50 / Силна дневна слънцезащита за лице

## Product Story / Routine
The site presents a 5-step skincare routine:
1. cleanser
2. rose water
3. anti-aging serum / youth elixir
4. moisturiser
5. SPF50 daily protection

## Key Themes Seen On Site
- natural and uncompromising care
- clean and effective products
- hydration
- glow / radiance
- anti-aging support
- antioxidant protection
- organic rose water
- prebiotic moisturising support
- daily SPF protection

## Social Proof Seen
Testimonials mention:
